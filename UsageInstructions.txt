1. As prompted by the program, enter the number units (in thousands) of each race for the inital 
state. 
    1-a) So if you wanted 12000 Dwarves, you would enter 12

2. Press any key to start as prompted

3. Observe as the years pass 
    3-a) Each normal year will be displayed for one second
    3-b) Each year on which something major happens will be displayed for three seconds
    3-c) The Graph of Middle Earth will show locations of each race throughout the world
    3-d) The bar graph will display the relative population levels of each race

4. Press enter on any year to exit the simulation and look at final statistics.
